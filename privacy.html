Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

Flynto respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website.

We are committed to being transparent and keeping your information safe.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information unless you voluntarily contact us (for example, via email or a contact form).

If you contact us, we may retain your message and email address solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not share this with third parties.

3. Third-Party Websites

Flynto is a directory that links to third-party AI tools and websites. When you click on a tool link, you will be redirected to an external website.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or policies of those third-party websites. We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of any external site you visit.

4. Cookies and Analytics

Cookies or similar tracking technologies may be used to improve user experience and analyze website traffic.

We may use anonymized analytics (such as page views and visitor counts) to understand how our directory is used. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

5. No Sale of Data

Flynto does not sell, trade, or rent any user data to third parties. We do not run advertisements or work with advertising networks that track users.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. By continuing to use Flynto, you agree to the current privacy policy.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via the Contact page.
